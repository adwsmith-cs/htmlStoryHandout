Chapter 10

by Quinn

Bob loves to golf after work. Bob loves golf so much that he plays every day after work. One time after work Bob was playing a round of golf per usual and got to the last hole. It was finally his turn to tee off when all of a sudden his golf club snapped in half! Bob couldnt believe it, in embarrassment he quit the round and then shortly after decided that golf was terrible and that he will never play the stupid sport again. He threw his bag of clubs in his fireplace and watched them burn to ashes.
